Culture

Silliman’s rich culture celebrates diversity, creativity, and heritage. From vibrant cultural festivals to artistic performances, the university nurtures an appreciation for the arts and traditions. This dynamic cultural environment fosters self-expression and a deep connection to both local and global identities, enriching the Silliman experience.

CULTURE HIGHLIGHTS

FRESHIE WALK -

DATE: August 15, 2024

ORGANIZER: Silliman University

INVOLVEMENT: Working Committee - 2

What? On August 15, 2024, I participated in the Freshie Walk event organized by Silliman University. This event is a special tradition for first-year students to officially kick off their journey at the university. As we arrived, the council asked for volunteers, and without knowing exactly what we were volunteering for, my friends and I eagerly raised our hands. To our surprise, we were tasked with handling the drums for the college chant! This was a first for us, and we had no prior experience with drums, so the situation was both exciting and nerve-wracking.


So What? Although we had no prior knowledge of drumming, the experience turned out to be much more fun than we expected. The energy of the Freshie Walk combined with our spontaneous drumming session created a unique and electrifying atmosphere. We felt part of something bigger, and the shared sense of excitement among us and our fellow students made the event memorable. This experience taught me how stepping out of my comfort zone can lead to unexpected yet rewarding moments, and how sometimes the most memorable experiences come from being thrown into new, challenging situations.


Now What? After this experience, I’m more open to embracing new opportunities, even if they seem unfamiliar or intimidating at first. I plan to take on more challenges throughout my college journey and look for ways to contribute, whether it’s participating in more activities or taking on leadership roles. I also realize the importance of teamwork, as our ability to work together in the moment played a huge role in making the event enjoyable.


Because of this activity, I am... more confident in taking on new experiences and less afraid to try something I’ve never done before. The Freshie Walk not only helped me bond with my classmates but also instilled a sense of pride and excitement in being a part of the Silliman University community.

KUWERDAS FILIPINAS SYMPHONIC RONDALLA -

DATE: Setember 13, 2024 7PM

ORGANIZER: Dr. Ramón P. Santos, National Artist for Music

INVOLVEMENT: Participant - (1 )

What? On Setyembre 13, 2024, I had the opportunity to attend Kuwerdas Filipinas Symphonic Rondalla, a live performance led by the renowned Dr. Ramón P. Santos, National Artist for Music. As someone new to live music shows, this experience was eye-opening and truly inspiring. The performance featured a symphonic rondalla, a unique and captivating blend of traditional Filipino instruments. What made this event stand out was not only the beautiful arrangements but also the creativity of the performers, who incorporated body movements like tapping instruments and stomping their feet to enhance the rhythm.


So What? The second piece of the performance, in particular, was my favorite. The dynamic use of instruments and the performers’ physicality added an unexpected energy that made the performance more immersive. The creativity of combining the traditional sounds with contemporary pop in the final performance was a highlight, especially the rendition of BINI's "Pantropiko." It was an exciting and engaging mix of modern and traditional, which energized the audience and gave a fresh twist to Filipino music. Stephany’s soprano performance was another standout moment, adding depth and elegance to the show.


Now What? Attending this event has deepened my appreciation for Filipino music and its ability to evolve and fuse with contemporary elements while staying rooted in tradition. I’m inspired by the creativity and passion that went into the performance and have realized how music can transcend genres and engage audiences in unexpected ways. Going forward, I want to explore more live performances to continue expanding my understanding and appreciation for different musical styles and cultural expressions.


Because of this activity, I am... more open to exploring different genres of music, especially live performances, and am inspired by the fusion of traditional and contemporary styles. The event not only showcased the richness of Filipino artistry but also reminded me how art can create unique and memorable experiences that stay with you long after the event is over.

RAMA, HARI -

DATE: October 5-6, 2024 | 7:00 PM

ORGANIZER:Silliman University Culture and Arts Council

INVOLVEMENT:Participant - 1

What? On October 5-6, 2024, at 7:00 PM, I attended Rama, Hari at the Claire Isabel McGill Luce Auditorium, organized by the Silliman University Culture and Arts Council. The performance blended dance, music, and drama, telling the epic story of Rama Hari through the works of five National Artists: Alice Reyes (choreography), Ryan Cayabyab (music), Bienvenido Lumbera (libretto), Salvador Bernal (production design), and Rolando Tinio (English translations). As a participant, my role was to watch and experience the performance, appreciating the collaboration of these artists to bring the story to life.

So What? I learned to appreciate ballet as an art form that tells a story through movement, music, and design. Initially, I expected a traditional ballet, but the use of shadow play during the final fight scene surprised me and added a new dimension to the performance. The creativity in blending different artistic elements left a strong impression on me. The most satisfying part was witnessing the choreography and the visual elements come together to convey a complex story. This experience reaffirmed my appreciation for the arts and how they can express deep, universal themes.

Now What? This experience has expanded my understanding of the performing arts, particularly ballet, as a powerful tool for storytelling. I can apply this new perspective by supporting and attending more performances, as well as seeking out diverse forms of art that communicate ideas and emotions in unique ways. I see the value of the arts in promoting culture and bringing people together, and I plan to continue exploring this interest by engaging with future performances and events.

Because of this activity, I am... more appreciative of the arts and how they enrich our understanding of culture, history, and human experience.
