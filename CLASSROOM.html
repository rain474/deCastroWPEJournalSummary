MILLER SOLUTIONS DEVELOPMENT INC. INDUSTRY LECTURE: "DESIGNING INTUITIVE USER EXPERIENCE: KEY UI/UX STRATEGIES -

DATE: Aug 22 2024 8:30 - 11:30 am

ORGANIZER: College of Computer Studies

INVOLVEMENT: Participant - (1 )

What? On August 22, 2024, from 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM, I attended the Miller Solutions Development Inc. Industry Lecture on "Designing Intuitive User Experience: Key UI/UX Strategies." The lecture was organized by the College of Computer Studies, where we were introduced to the fundamental principles of UI/UX design. As first-year students, it was our first time diving into the significance of user-centered design and how it impacts the user’s experience with products. The session highlighted the importance of making interfaces simple, consistent, and empathetic to user needs, which was a new and valuable perspective for us.

So What? This lecture opened my eyes to how crucial good design is in creating seamless user experiences. I was surprised at how even small design changes could greatly improve how intuitive a website or app feels. What stood out to me the most was how empathy is a central part of the design process—understanding the users’ needs and challenges can lead to creating more accessible and user-friendly products. The most satisfying part of the event was the hands-on activity where we were challenged to design our own website interface. It made the experience more interactive and helped me understand the real-world application of what we had learned.

Now What? This experience has inspired me to pursue further knowledge in UI/UX design. I plan to explore more about user-centered design principles and consider how to apply them in my own projects. I am now more interested in the design aspect of technology, not just the development, and I will focus on enhancing my skills in creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces. If I could attend a similar lecture again, I would love to dive deeper into the different design tools and software used by professionals.

Because of this activity, I am... more motivated to learn about UI/UX design and excited to apply the principles of empathy, simplicity, and consistency in creating intuitive digital experiences.
